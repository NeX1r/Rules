Rules

  1. Advertising in any form is prohibited. It is forbidden to promote, hint at, or demonstrate third-party resources, brands, services, servers, channels, groups, products, or projects. This includes direct and indirect mentions, logos, images, videos, voice messages, QR codes, shortened links, status texts, nicknames, avatars, banners, and even "accidental mentions." Even if your content is not clickable or is “blurred” — it is still considered advertising. Attempts to mask or distort links, write them with spaces, screenshots, ASCII art, or use other tricks are violations.
    Punishment: Permanent ban without warnings.
  2. NSFW and NSFL content is prohibited. Any erotic, pornographic, shocking, or disturbing material is prohibited. This includes nudity, sexual content, hentai, gore, dismemberment, suicide, corpses, torture, cruelty, shocking photos/videos, and disturbing sounds. Prohibited formats: text, images, GIFs, videos, voice messages, links, ASCII art, memes, and stickers. Even stylized, "artistic," humorous, or censored depictions are still considered violations.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  3. Spam and flooding are prohibited. Spam is sending repeated or meaningless content (messages, emojis, characters, mentions, images, sounds, or links) in a short time. Flooding is overloading the chat with massive messages, voice disruptions, loud noises in voice channels, or repeated joins/leaves. "Just testing," "just trolling," or "copy-paste" is still considered spam/flood.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 30 min; 2nd — mute 1 day; repeat — 7 days ban.
  4. Begging is prohibited. Requests for money, gifts, items, accounts, services, or donations are prohibited — even if it’s “as a joke” or “ironically.” Asking in statuses, private chats, or disguised as humor is still considered begging.
    Punishment: Warn → repeat — mute 12h → 3 warns = 7 days ban.
  5. Gambling for money or valuables is prohibited. Prohibited: bets, lotteries, raffles, casino-style games, or challenges involving real/virtual money, accounts, gift codes, or valuable in-game items. Even "just for fun" raffles with an entry fee are banned.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  6. Sharing personal information and blackmail are prohibited. Sharing any personal data (yours or someone else’s) is strictly forbidden. This includes names, addresses, phone numbers, emails, IPs, photos, documents, private conversations, and passwords — regardless of whether the data is publicly available elsewhere. Threatening to reveal or use such data counts as blackmail.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  7. Bypassing restrictions is prohibited. Forbidden: using alternate accounts, borrowed accounts, or technical tricks to avoid bans, mutes, or role restrictions. Attempting to rejoin after being kicked, VPN evasion, or impersonating other members counts as bypassing.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  8. Hate speech or provocation based on discrimination is prohibited. Insults or mockery based on race, nationality, religion, gender, age, appearance, orientation, profession, disabilities, or other traits are strictly forbidden. This includes direct speech, memes, jokes, "sarcasm," or indirect hints.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 1 day; 2nd — mute 7 days; repeat — permanent ban.
  9. Unjustified tagging of staff is prohibited. Staff (admins, mods, developers) may only be tagged in serious cases: technical problems, violations, or real emergencies. Any other tagging (jokes, spam, “just checking”) is forbidden.
    Punishment: Warn → repeat — mute 12h.
  10. Insulting the administration, developers, or editors is prohibited. Attempts to humiliate, mock, or discredit the project staff in any form (direct, indirect, hints, memes) are forbidden.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 3 days; 2nd — 30 days ban; repeat — permanent ban.
  11. Copying someone else’s profile is prohibited. Using nicknames, avatars, statuses, banners, or other attributes to impersonate another member is forbidden. Even partial copying (similar names with symbols or mirrored avatars) counts as impersonation.
    Punishment: Mute until profile change → repeat — 7 days ban.
  12. Provoking personal conflicts is prohibited. Starting or fueling quarrels, personal attacks, insults about private lives, or dragging private conflicts into public spaces is not allowed.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 12h; 2nd — mute 3 days; repeat — 14 days ban.
  13. Political discussion is prohibited. Any political topics (parties, figures, events, slogans, or symbols) are not allowed, regardless of whether the mention is positive, negative, neutral, “joking,” or “just sharing news.”
    Punishment: Warn → mute 1 day → repeat — 7 days ban.
  14. Insulting relatives and aggressive behavior are prohibited. Insulting or mocking family, relatives, or close ones, as well as threats, intimidation, or pressure are strictly forbidden.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 3 days; 2nd — 14 days ban; repeat — permanent ban.
  15. Buying, selling, or trading is prohibited. Any commercial activity is forbidden: buying, selling, exchanging goods, accounts, services, codes, in-game items, or money. Even “friendly exchanges” are still counted as trade.
    Punishment: Warn → mute 3 days → repeat — permanent ban.

Notes and Interpretations

  • Any violation in a disguised or indirect form is still a violation.
  • Saying "it was a joke" does not exempt from responsibility.
  • If a situation is not explicitly described, staff has the right to decide based on similar existing rules.
  • The administration reserves the right to change the rules without prior notice.
  • Not knowing the rules does not exempt from responsibility.
  • 3 warns = automatic ban.
