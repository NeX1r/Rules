Rules

  1. Advertising without permission is prohibited. Promoting third-party resources, brands, services, or projects without admin consent is forbidden. This includes links, logos, QR codes, and mentions in messages or profiles. Accidental mentions or jokes are allowed if they are not blatant advertising.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  2. NSFW and NSFL content is restricted. Posting explicitly pornographic, shocking, or violent content (porn, gore, dismemberment, torture) is prohibited. Light memes with erotic undertones or "artistic" images are allowed if they stay within the bounds of decency (at moderators' discretion).
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  3. Spam and flooding are undesirable. Do not send repetitive or meaningless messages, emojis, images, or links. Avoid overloading the chat with excessive messages or noise in voice channels.
    Punishment: Warning → mute for 30 minutes → repeat — mute for 12 hours.
  4. Begging is not welcome. Do not ask for money, gifts, items, or services. Joking requests are allowed if they do not annoy other participants.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  5. Gambling is undesirable. Betting or lotteries involving real/virtual money or valuable items are prohibited. Games "for fun" without a financial component are allowed.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  6. Personal information is protected. Do not share personal data (yours or others') without permission: addresses, phone numbers, photos, documents. Threats to disclose data are considered blackmail.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  7. Bypassing restrictions is prohibited. Do not use alternate accounts or VPNs to evade bans or mutes.
    Punishment: Mute or ban depending on the underlying punishment.
  8. Respect others. Insults or mockery based on race, gender, religion, orientation, or other characteristics are prohibited. Jokes are allowed if they do not cross into insults (at moderators' discretion).
    Punishment: Warning → mute for 12 hours → repeat — mute for 3 days.
  9. Mention staff only for valid reasons. Tag the administration only for real issues (technical problems, violations). Joking mentions are undesirable.
    Punishment: Warning → mute for 6 hours.
  10. Respect the administration. Do not insult or mock administrators, moderators, or developers. Constructive criticism is allowed.
    Punishment: Warning → mute for 1 day → repeat — ban for 7 days.
  11. Do not copy others' profiles. Do not use nicknames, avatars, or statuses to impersonate another participant.
    Punishment: Warning → mute until profile change.
  12. Avoid conflicts. Do not start quarrels or engage in personal attacks. Resolve personal conflicts in private chats.
    Punishment: Warning → mute for 12 hours → repeat — mute for 3 days.
  13. Politics is restricted. Avoid discussing political topics (parties, events, figures). Joking memes are allowed if they do not provoke conflicts.
    Punishment: Warning → mute for 12 hours → repeat — mute for 3 days.
  14. Aggression is unacceptable. Insulting relatives, threats, or intimidation are prohibited.
    Punishment: Mute for 1 day → repeat — ban for 7 days.
  15. Trading is undesirable. Do not engage in buying, selling, or exchanging goods, accounts, or services. Friendly in-game exchanges are allowed with moderators' permission.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.

Notes

  • Jokes and informal content are allowed if they do not violate respect for other participants.
  • Moderators may make decisions in ambiguous situations based on common sense.
  • The administration may update the rules, notifying participants in the chat.
  • 3 warnings = mute for 3 days, 5 warnings = ban.
