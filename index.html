Rules

  1. Advertising in any form is prohibited. This includes any mention, display, or hint towards third-party resources, brands, products, services, servers, groups, channels, etc.
    Punishment: Permanent ban without warnings.
  2. NSFW and NSFL content is prohibited. This includes sexual materials, pornography, depictions of violence, corpses, torture, cruelty, or shocking content.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  3. Spam and flooding are prohibited.
    Punishment: 1st time — mute 30 min; 2nd — mute 1 day; repeat — 7 days ban.
  4. Begging is prohibited.
    Punishment: Warn → repeat — mute 12h → 3 warns = 7 days ban.
  5. Gambling for money or valuables is prohibited.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  6. Sharing personal information and blackmail are prohibited.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  7. Bypassing restrictions is prohibited.
    Punishment: Permanent ban.
  8. Hate speech or provocation based on discrimination is prohibited.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 1 day; 2nd — mute 7 days; repeat — permanent ban.
  9. Unjustified tagging of staff is prohibited.
    Punishment: Warn → repeat — mute 12h.
  10. Insulting the administration, developers, or editors is prohibited.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 3 days; 2nd — 30 days ban; repeat — permanent ban.
  11. Copying someone else’s profile is prohibited.
    Punishment: Mute until profile change → repeat — 7 days ban.
  12. Provoking personal conflicts is prohibited.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 12h; 2nd — mute 3 days; repeat — 14 days ban.
  13. Political discussion is prohibited.
    Punishment: Warn → mute 1 day → repeat — 7 days ban.
  14. Insulting relatives and aggressive behavior are prohibited.
    Punishment: 1st — mute 3 days; 2nd — 14 days ban; repeat — permanent ban.
  15. Buying, selling, or trading is prohibited.
    Punishment: Warn → mute 3 days → repeat — permanent ban.

Notes and Interpretations

  • Any violation in a disguised or indirect form is still a violation.
  • Saying "it was a joke" does not exempt from responsibility.
  • If a situation is not explicitly described, staff has the right to decide based on similar existing rules.
  • The administration reserves the right to change the rules without prior notice.
  • Not knowing the rules does not exempt from responsibility.
  • 3 warns = automatic ban.
